Estimating the Impact of Education on Earnings: A Causal Tree Based Analysis of Conditional Average Treatment Effects

This project exaimines the casual relationship between education and earning based on the casual tree approach. The findings of this study will contribute to the literature on the causal impact of education on labor market outcomes and inform guardians on the potential benefits of education investments.
